## About

This module is designed to equip you with a comprehensive understanding of error handling in Express applications. Focused on practical techniques, it guides you through basic concepts such as developing error pages and utilizing flash messages. You'll also explore advanced topics like the use of HTTP status codes for precise error communication and resolving port conflicts. Designed for learners at various levels, this module leaves you well equipped to build robust, user-friendly Express applications with stable and reliable functionality.
